\BackBody{This book is a selection of the papers presented at Methods
in Dialectology XV, held in Groningen, the Netherlands, 11-15 August 2014.
While the focus is on methodology, the volume also includes specialized
studies on varieties of Catalan, Breton, Croatian, (Belgian) Dutch, English
(in the US, the UK and in Japan), German (including Swiss German), Italian
(including Tyrolean Italian), Japanese, and Spanish as well as on heritage
languages in Canada.}
